pyrogue

of http://spielend-programmieren.at

graphics:
spritesheets are taken from the free / libre open-source game project
dungeon crawl stone soup, see https://crawl.develz.org/
the licenses of those spritesheets are, as far as i understand:
http://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/
see https://code.google.com/p/crawl-tiles/ for more details

graphics (portraits) and sound effects and music:
taken from the free / libre open-source game project Battle of Wesnoth,
see http://www.wesnoth.org/
the licenses of those graphic and sound / music art assets are,
as far as i understand:
gpl 2, http://www.gnu.org/licenses/old-licenses/gpl-2.0.html
see http://wiki.wesnoth.org/Wesnoth:Copyrights for more details
